About Hunter x Hunter

"Hunter x Hunter" (ハンター×ハンター, Hantā Hantā) is a 2011 Japanese animated adventure television series based on the manga by Yoshihiro Togashi. The story follows Gon Freecss, a young boy who dreams of becoming a Hunter—a prestigious title given to those who excel in tracking down treasures, rare beasts, and even individuals. Gon’s primary motivation is to find his estranged father, Ging Freecss, who is a legendary Hunter himself. Along his journey, Gon befriends and teams up with other aspiring Hunters, including Killua Zoldyck, a skilled assassin from a notorious family, and others who face a series of challenging and dangerous tasks.
