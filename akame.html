About Akame ga kill

"Akame ga Kill!" (アカメが斬る!, Akame ga Kiru!) is a 2014 Japanese animated dark fantasy television series adapted from the manga by Takahiro and illustrated by Tetsuya Tashiro. The story follows Tatsumi, a young swordsman from a rural village who travels to the capital city with dreams of earning money to save his homeland. Upon discovering the capital’s corruption and the cruelty of its ruling class, Tatsumi joins Night Raid, a group of assassins aiming to overthrow the corrupt government and bring justice to the oppressed.
