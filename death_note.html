About Death note

"Death Note" (デスノート, Desu Nōto) is a 2006 Japanese animated psychological thriller television series based on the manga by Tsugumi Ohba and Takeshi Obata. The series follows Light Yagami, a highly intelligent high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook that allows him to kill anyone simply by writing their name in it. As Light adopts the persona of "Kira" and seeks to create a utopia free of crime, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes cat-and-mouse game with law enforcement and a brilliant but enigmatic detective known only as "L."
