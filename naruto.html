About Naruto

"Naruto" (ナルト) is a 2002 Japanese animated action-adventure television series adapted from the manga by Masashi Kishimoto. The story centers around Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest leader of his village, known as the Hokage. Orphaned and shunned by his village due to having a powerful demon fox sealed within him, Naruto strives to gain recognition and prove his worth. Alongside his friends and fellow ninjas, such as Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno, Naruto embarks on numerous missions, faces formidable foes, and uncovers the mysteries surrounding his past and his village’s hidden secrets.
