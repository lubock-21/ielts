About jujutsu kaisen

"Jujutsu Kaisen" (呪術廻戦) is a 2020 Japanese animated dark fantasy television series based on the manga of the same name by Gege Akutami. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who, after consuming a cursed object containing the powerful Curse known as Ryomen Sukuna, becomes entangled in the world of Jujutsu Sorcery. He joins a school of Jujutsu Sorcerers to fight against malevolent spirits and unravel the mysteries of the curse that now resides within him.
