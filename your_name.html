About Your Name

"Your Name" (君の名は。, Kimi no Na wa) is a 2016 Japanese animated romantic fantasy film directed by Makoto Shinkai. It tells the story of a high school girl in rural Japan and a high school boy in Tokyo who mysteriously begin to swap bodies.

The film explores themes of love, time, and memory, and has been praised for its stunning animation and emotional depth. It became one of the highest-grossing anime films of all time and received numerous awards and nominations.
