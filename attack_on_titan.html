About jujutsu kaisen

"Attack on Titan" (進撃の巨人, Shingeki no Kyojin) is a 2013 Japanese animated dark fantasy television series adapted from the manga by Hajime Isayama. Set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to gigantic humanoid creatures known as Titans, the series follows Eren Yeager, his adoptive sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their friend Armin Arlert. After their home is destroyed by a Titan, they enlist in the military to fight back and uncover the secrets behind the Titans and their own world.
