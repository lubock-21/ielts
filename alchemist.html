About Fullmetal Alchemist

"Fullmetal Alchemist" (鋼の錬金術師, Hagane no Renkinjutsushi) is a 2003 Japanese animated fantasy television series adapted from the manga by Hiromu Arakawa. The story follows Edward and Alphonse Elric, two brothers who commit the taboo of human transmutation in an attempt to bring their deceased mother back to life. The ritual goes horribly wrong, resulting in Edward losing an arm and a leg, and Alphonse losing his entire body, with his soul bound to a suit of armor. To restore their bodies, they embark on a quest to find the Philosopher's Stone, a legendary artifact that is said to amplify alchemical abilities and potentially undo their transmutation mishap.
