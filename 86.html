About 86

"86" (エイティシックス, Eiti Shikkusu) is a 2021 Japanese animated science fiction television series adapted from the light novel series by Asato Asato and illustrated by Shirabii. The story is set in a dystopian future where the Republic of San Magnolia is engaged in a protracted war with the neighboring Empire of Giad. The Republic, which claims to be a land of equality, hides the brutal reality of its conflict by using unmanned drones known as "Juggernauts" to fight the war. In truth, these drones are piloted by the oppressed 86, a group of young people from the war-torn 86th Sector who are treated as expendable soldiers.
