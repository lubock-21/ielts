About Blue Lock

"Blue Lock" (ブルーロック, Burū Rokku) is a 2022 Japanese animated sports drama series adapted from the manga by Muneyuki Kaneshiro and illustrated by Yusuke Nomura. The story centers on Yoichi Isagi, a high school soccer player who, after missing out on a chance to lead his team to victory, is recruited into the Blue Lock program. This highly competitive training camp aims to create Japan’s best striker by pitting the nation’s top young talents against each other in a series of rigorous and often brutal challenges.
